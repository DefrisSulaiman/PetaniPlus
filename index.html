About PetaniPlus

PetaniPlus is revolutionizing Indonesia's agricultural supply chain by creating direct connections between farmers and buyers. Our platform eliminates unnecessary intermediaries, ensuring farmers receive fair prices while consumers access fresh, quality produce. Through advanced technology, we provide real-time market data, crop tracking systems, and transparent pricing mechanisms. Our mission is to build a sustainable agricultural ecosystem that benefits all stakeholders while promoting food security and economic prosperity for farming communities.
